Abstract

There are many things that affect the lives of people. Money and its flow through the population of the United States determines how people live. It is measurable and the primary tool of the governments in the United States to protect and improve the lives of its people. This study is to model the flow of money through the United Stated economy for the purpose to determine what can be done to improve the quality of life for all people while at the same time encourage people to strive to do better and in general make the country better.
