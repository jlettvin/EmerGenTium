Abstract

There are many things that affect the lives of people. Money and its flow between people, businesses and the government of the United States primarily determines how people live. It has been measured over an extended period of time. This study is to use this data to build a model of the flow of money through the United States economy. Then this model can be used to determine what can be done to improve the quality of life for people of all economic levels while at the same time encourage eveyone to strive to do better and in general make the country better.
