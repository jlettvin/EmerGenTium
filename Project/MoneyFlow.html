Identify the Flow of Money

Identify the methods that money flows among people, businesses and governments.

Money flows that are unmanageable:
Salaries, Social Security or other fixed income
Income from investments
Acquisition of necessities such as food and home
Entertainment
Money flows that are manageable:
Taxes
Interest rates
Social Security
Got to be more!
Policies and Incentives:
Patents and Copyrights
Awards and credit for accomplishments

Contributions to non-profit organizations such as churches, United Fund, Go Fund Me etc.
Working for non-profit groups
Aid to individuals in need

Survival
